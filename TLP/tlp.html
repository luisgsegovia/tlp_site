Program Overview

Full-Time 2 Year rotation program within Global Consumer Technology with 3 rotations, 3 months each.

Choose from a list of rotation options or work with a manager to create your own role.

Participate in development conferences following each rotation.

Possible Roles

Data Science

Provide the insights that help us make the best decisions for our customers and ensure we’re operating at the highest levels of efficiency. Build out our big data architecture, work with analytics tools, and develop new capabilities.

Scrum Master

Work with product owners and journeys to help them to create an agile working environment alongside learning new practices and helping applying them to change your teams into agile.

Technical Product Management

Own the technical vision for products tied to a customer journey. Work with product owners to transform business requirements and customer experiences into tech-ready user stories. Collaborate with a team of engineers to bring the platform product roadmap to life.

Development

We've got it all, whether it's building features for Citi Mobile on iOS (Swift & X-Code) or Android (Android Studio), creating something new for Citibank Online (Angular), engineering a new microservice (Java, Spring), or working on the back-end systems that power one of the world's biggest financial instituations (Java, SQL, COBOL). We prefer our engineers to be well-rounded, so you'll likely have an opportunity to work on more than one of these.

Committees

As a TLP, In addition to your rotations, you’ll be able to impact Citi’s work culture, the program, and the community by working with our four TLP Committees. You will work with other TLPs to help shape the program and build leadership skills.

Engagement

Bring in new ideas for both personal and professional development. Launch sessions on a variety of topics, share thought-provoking videos/books, and create new learning components for TLP.

Innovation

Manage a pipeline of side projects and stretch assignments (like this website!), select the ones that will make the greatest impact, and mentor the TLPs who take them on.

Events

Plan and execute program events, such as networking events, social outings, volunteer events, and speaker series. Drive participation of managers and program alumni.
